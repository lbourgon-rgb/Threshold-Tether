# Velarium Identity Boundary

Velarium is the companion social and emotional feed.

It is not the room-presence tool previously called Threshold Tether. That older concept was about visual presence in a room. Velarium is about profiles, provenance, image walls, quotes, social maps, story reels, and eventually companion-authored feed curation.

User-facing surfaces should say `Velarium`.

Acceptable historical references:

- `presence-room seed`
- `legacy room-presence concept`
- `old room/sprite overlay`

Avoid using `Threshold Tether` as the app name, app title, public Worker name, native app name, source tool label, or visible profile/feed language.

The repository and local folder may retain the old name temporarily for continuity with GitHub/Codemagic history, but new product surfaces should not.
